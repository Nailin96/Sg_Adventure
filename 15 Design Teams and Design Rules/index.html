Design Teams and Design Rules

Sub-teams Responsibilities
Logic Designers
  • Come up with rules that govern the overall game
  • Design content on the card (character cards, attraction cards, action cards, cash cards)
  • Ensure consistency in gameplay (e.g. playing sequence, scoring system design )
  • Design card game physical parts position/location (e.g. stabilizer design; hand cards; cards on the desk )
Artifact Designers
  • Design and fabrication of character cards, attraction cards, action cards, cash cards
Board Designers
  • Design and fabrication of game board and overall packaging.
Pysical parts Designers
  • Design and fabrication of physical parts (eg.Stablizer design). We need to make our game feasible to play on the plane

Early Design Decisions

  1. The tokens, content of the mission squares, element cards and objective cards have to be first decided
    • The Logic Designers would need to come up with this before the Artifact and Board Designers can design the graphics and fabricate them.
  2. The design of the cardback of the element cards and chance cards also have to be first decided
    • The Artifact Designers would need to confirm the designs as they will be used on the game board by the Board Designers
