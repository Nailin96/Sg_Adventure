Design Teams and Design Rules

Sub-teams Responsibilities
Logic Designers
  • Come up with rules that govern the overall game
  • Design content on the card (character cards, attraction cards, action cards, cash cards)
  • Ensure consistency in gameplay (e.g. playing sequence, scoring system design )
  • Design card game physical parts position/location (e.g. stabilizer design; hand cards; cards on the desk )
Artifact Designers
  • Design and fabrication of character cards, attraction cards, action cards, cash cards
Cards package Designers
  • Design and fabrication of cards and overall packaging.
Physical parts Designers
  • Design and fabrication of physical parts (eg.Stabilizer design). We need to make our game feasible to play on the plane

Early Design Decisions

  1. The design of the content of the character cards, attraction cards, action cards, cash cards need to came up first.
    • The Logic Designers would need to come up with the rules before the Artifact and Package Designers can design the graphics and fabricate them.
    • The Physical parts designer can design the stabilizer simultaneously with the Artifact designers and Cards package designers.
