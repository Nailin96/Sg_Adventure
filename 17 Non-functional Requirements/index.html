Non-functional Requirements

  1. The card game shall be waterproof.
  2. The card game packaging shall be small, at most 3200cm3.
  3. The card game shall not be blown away by the wind or fall off when plane is not stable. Hence it should not be too light.
  4. The cards shall be recyclable.
  5. The cards shall not contain any sharp metal edges.
  6. Prototyping costs for the board game shall not exceed $200.
  7. The cards shall be replaceable.
  8. The cards shall be sufficient to fulfill the need of different types of travellers.
  9. A single round of the game shall take at most half an hour to complete, with 90% confidence.
  10. The card game should not require much place to play.
  11. The card game should be able to be brought on plane.
